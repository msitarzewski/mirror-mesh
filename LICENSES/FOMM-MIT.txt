The following files vendored under models/external/fomm/ are derived from the
First Order Motion Model for Image Animation by Aliaksandr Siarohin et al.

Upstream:        https://github.com/AliaksandrSiarohin/first-order-model
Upstream commit: c0274845cb2dd8f0f2fe6da580d97b60fef90c91 (fetched 2026-05-19)

Vendored files:
  models/external/fomm/keypoint_detector.py  (from modules/keypoint_detector.py)
  models/external/fomm/dense_motion.py       (from modules/dense_motion.py)
  models/external/fomm/generator.py          (from modules/generator.py)
  models/external/fomm/util.py               (from modules/util.py)

Modifications:
  - Headers added to each vendored file noting origin, commit, license.
  - util.py: SynchronizedBatchNorm2d (multi-GPU training primitive from the
    sync_batchnorm extension) replaced with torch.nn.BatchNorm2d. Numerically
    identical at batch_size=1 inference, and the form coremltools converts.
    No other architectural changes.
