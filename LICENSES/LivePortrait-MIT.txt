Modifications:
  - Attribution header prepended to each vendored .py noting origin, commit,
    license, and (where applicable) the InsightFace research-only caveat.
  - No architectural changes. Unlike the FOMM vendor at models/external/fomm/,
    LivePortrait's util.py already uses stock torch.nn.BatchNorm2d /
    BatchNorm3d / InstanceNorm2d / LayerNorm — no SyncBatchNorm patching was
    needed.

The InsightFace research-only restriction that LivePortrait's LICENSE
appends regarding runtime face-detection weights is reproduced verbatim in
LICENSES/InsightFace-research-only.txt, alongside the ADR-0015 framing that
makes that restriction compatible with MirrorMesh's research posture.
